How to play

Buildr is a game where you can build anything you want with cubes with different colors and textures. Let your imagination take over and build something amazing with Buildr.

  • Add cube

  • Add a cube to the playground and click the icon in the nav to change the color or texture of the cube.

  • Add object

  • Add object to the playground by clicking the icon in the nav.

  • Keybindings

    • Add cube

    • Erase cube

    • Change view

    • Zoom

    You can find all the keybindings by clicking the icon in the nav.

  • You wanna challenge?

    • Challenge yourself by limiting your time to build and set max number of cubes.
